Account & Data Deletion

Effective date: 7 May 2026  |  Version 1.0

You have the right to delete your DiabEasy account and all associated personal data at any time, free of charge, with no questions asked. This page explains how.

Option 1 — Delete directly inside the app (fastest)

If you still have access to your DiabEasy account, this is the quickest way:

1Open DiabEasy
Launch the app on your device and make sure you are signed in to the account you want to delete.
2Go to Profile → Settings tab
Tap the Profile tab in the bottom navigation bar, then switch to the Settings tab at the top.
3Scroll to Account Deletion
Scroll down to the Account Deletion section and tap Delete My Account.
4Confirm deletion
Confirm when prompted. Your account, profile, all glucose readings, insulin logs, meal history, and caregiver codes will be permanently deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Submit a deletion request by email

If you no longer have access to the app or your account, email us and we will process the deletion within 30 days.

Please include your registered email address and, if available, your Firebase UID (visible in Profile → Settings).
